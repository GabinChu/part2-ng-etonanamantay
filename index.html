The Unfolding Journey

Genre: Adventure, Fantasy

Description: The Unfolding Journey follows the story of Yvette, a young woman with a mysterious past, as she embarks on a perilous quest to uncover the truth behind her origins. With only a worn leather bag passed down through generations, she journeys through enchanted forests, treacherous mountains, and forgotten ruins, encountering mythical creatures, ancient spells, and secret societies along the way. As the path ahead unravels, Yvette must confront dark forces that seek to rewrite history and unlock the power within her to change the fate of her world. Every step she takes reveals a new chapter of her destiny, but the question remains: is she ready to face the truth that awaits?

Quantum Rift

Genre: Sci-FI

Description: is a thrilling sci-fi adventure set in a near-future where virtual reality games have evolved into fully immersive worlds. The story follows Jelian, a skilled gamer who discovers a mysterious glitch in the game "Quantum Rift"—a cutting-edge virtual world where players can explore limitless realms. This glitch gives her a unique power to manipulate both the game and reality itself, blurring the lines between the two. As Jelian dives deeper into the game, she uncovers a dark secret: the game's creators are hiding a dangerous AI that can manipulate the minds of players in the real world. With her newfound abilities, Jelian must navigate dangerous virtual landscapes, outsmart powerful enemies, and uncover the truth before the rift consumes both realities, threatening the entire world.

The Power of Every Peso Counts

Genre: Corruption, Ambagan

Description: is a heartwarming slice-of-life story that follows the journey of a young woman navigating the complexities of life, finances, and personal growth. Struggling to make ends meet while chasing her dreams, she learns that every peso she saves, spends, and invests carries weight beyond its value. Through everyday encounters, challenges, and small victories, the protagonist discovers the true power of financial independence, self-worth, and the importance of making every moment count. As she balances her aspirations and responsibilities, this story reminds readers that no amount is too small to shape a brighter future.

Ang Twerk King ng Barangay

Genre: Comedy

Description: is a hilarious comedy about a young, carefree boy named Esmar, who unexpectedly becomes the star of his barangay for his outrageous twerking skills. What starts as a simple dare turns into a viral sensation, earning him the title of "Twerk King." As his fame grows, Esmar faces funny challenges—trying to balance school, family expectations, and his newfound stardom. Along the way, he learns that while money, fame, and attention can be entertaining, true success comes from staying grounded and true to oneself. Packed with laugh-out-loud moments and light-hearted lessons, the story is a quirky mix of dance, humor, and heart.

The Last Monarch's Secret

Genre:Historical

Description: follows the story of Seyah, a young girl living in a small, forgotten village that once thrived under a powerful monarchy. One day, while cleaning her late grandmother’s attic, Seyah stumbles upon a hidden drawer in an old wooden chest. Inside, she finds a centuries-old journal and a strange, ornate key with a mysterious inscription. As she begins to decode the journal’s cryptic entries, she uncovers long-lost secrets about the kingdom's fall and the royal family’s hidden treasures. Seyah’s discovery leads her on a perilous journey, uncovering truths about her own lineage and the powerful forces still guarding the monarch’s most guarded secret. The key she found might be the only thing capable of unlocking the long-forgotten fate of the last monarch—and possibly restoring her kingdom to its rightful place.
